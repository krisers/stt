A chaotic mix of drama, comedy, and ambition unfolds as Fran, a nanny, unexpectedly auditions for a Shakespeare play and lands the role of Juliet. With the help (or hindrance) of her employer, Maxwell, and the disapproving presence of her former flame, Niles, Fran navigates the challenges of theater, family responsibilities, and personal growth. As the weekend approaches, secrets about the play's casting and motivations threaten to sabotage the production, leading to hilarious and heartfelt moments.
