In a comical and dramatic turn, Fran, driven by a desire for adventure, decides to audition for a Shakespearean play and plans to escape her mundane life, much to the disapproval of her husband, Maxwell. With Niles stepping in to play Romeo, unexpected comedic interactions and a secret plan to sabotage the production unfold, raising questions about true talent and hidden motives. As Fran's determination clashes with the realities of parenting and personal aspirations, the stakes are set for a hilarious and heartfelt journey.
