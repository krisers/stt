A group of friends from a small town are haunted by a dark secret from their past, involving a car accident that resulted in the death of a man named David Egan. As they struggle to cope with the guilt and fear, they receive a mysterious letter, leading them to suspect someone from their circle might be seeking revenge. As the Fourth of July approaches, they race against time to uncover the truth and confront their past, while a series of terrifying events threaten to expose their secrets and destroy their lives forever.
