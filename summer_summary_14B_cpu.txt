A group of friends from a small town revisit their past during a Fourth of July celebration, when a series of mysterious events and secrets from the previous summer threaten to destroy their lives. As they try to uncover the truth behind a murder and a series of strange occurrences, they must confront their own guilt and the possibility that one of them might be responsible for more than they initially thought. With danger lurking around every corner, their friendships are tested, and they struggle to find redemption and closure before it's too late.
