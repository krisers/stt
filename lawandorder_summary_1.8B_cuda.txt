Detective Milgram impersonates Merritt Rook, a high school student accused of murder, and uses a staged assassination to expose the dangerous cult-like beliefs of the fast food chain, HappiBurger, which he infiltrates to manipulate employees into committing crimes. After revealing the plot to Merritt, Detective Milgram confesses to participating in the fake shootings and implicates the management of HappiBurger in his scheme. This trial demonstrates the dangers of conformity and the consequences of ignoring the voices within one's community. Detective Milgram's actions lead to the conviction of Merritt and the dismantling of HappiBurger as a cult organization.
