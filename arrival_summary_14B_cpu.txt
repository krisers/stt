In a world torn by the sudden arrival of 12 mysterious objects, linguist Louise Banks races against time to communicate with extraterrestrial beings, decoding their message and uncovering the truth behind their visit, while grappling with personal revelations and the potential consequences of humanity's encounter. As nations teeter on the brink of war, Louise's journey challenges the boundaries of language, understanding, and the meaning of destiny.
