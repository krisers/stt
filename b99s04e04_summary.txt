In the midst of a night shift filled with humor and camaraderie, Jake Peralta returns to work after an absence, bringing with him a newfound determination to solve a case. As the team faces challenges, personal issues, and unexpected distractions, they must confront their own morale and adapt to the dynamics of the night shift, all while trying to maintain their detective skills and solve the mystery of a stolen jewelry heist. Will the Night Boys (or should they be called the Dark Stallions?) triumph over the odds and rediscover their old camaraderie, or will the night shift continue to test them? Find out in this hilarious and heartwarming episode of Brooklyn Nine-Nine.
