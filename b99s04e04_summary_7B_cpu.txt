In a humorous and dramatic turn, "WWW.MY-SUBS.COM" follows the story of detective Jake Peralta, who returns from medical leave with a newfound obsession with retro tips on his shoes, much to the dismay of his colleagues. Despite their intervention, Jake continues to embrace the quirky habit, leading to comedic mishaps and a team intervention. As Jake tries to reconnect with his partner Boyle and restore their crime-solving dynamics, personal changes and unexpected challenges arise, testing their friendship and the department's morale. Will Jake's unusual approach to work lead to success or further complications? Find out in this lighthearted cop drama.
