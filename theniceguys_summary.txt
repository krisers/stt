A group of friends spends their day at the city pool, discussing mundane topics like towels and jobs. However, their lives take a dramatic turn when they encounter a private investigator, Jackson Healy, who is involved in a case involving a missing person and potential corruption. Amidst the chaos, Healy discovers a web of lies and murder surrounding a famous adult film star, Misty Mountains, and the influential people involved. As the investigation unfolds, Healy and the characters navigate personal connections, secrets, and the possibility of redemption.
