Detective Stabler finds Merritt Rook guilty of murder for attempting to kill Dr. Francis Slifkin, the audio engineer, by administering electric shocks to his wife and her baby. Merritt, a former engineer turned criminal, impersonates a police officer and takes control of the situation, forcing Stabler to intervene and ultimately discover that he has faked his own arrest in an attempt to protect his wife and son. The jury finds Merritt guilty and sends him to jail, while Stabler's actions lead to a turning point for the community and highlight the dangers of conformity and obedience within society.
