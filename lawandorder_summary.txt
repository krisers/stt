In "HappiBurger," a group of criminals target a fast-food restaurant by pretending to be police officers. When they try to force a teenager into a strip search, a real detective intervenes and reveals the identity of the perpetrators. A group of activists, led by Merritt Rook, protests against authority and corruption in society, and later confronts the criminals in a dramatic showdown. The story highlights themes of power dynamics, obedience, and the consequences of blindly following authority.
