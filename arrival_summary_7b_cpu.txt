In a world where memory doesn't function as expected, a character named Louise Banks grapples with the blurred lines between beginnings and endings. As she investigates mysterious alien interactions, she uncovers secrets that challenge her understanding of time, language, and the nature of communication. With the world on the brink of a global crisis, Louise must confront personal demons and make choices that could determine the fate of humanity, all while searching for the truth behind the beings they encounter.
