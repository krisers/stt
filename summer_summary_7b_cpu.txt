In a suspenseful tale set against the backdrop of a Fourth of July celebration, a group of friends grapple with the truth behind a fatal accident that they initially believed was an unfortunate accident. As they uncover dark secrets and a possible connection to a missing person, they must confront their own guilt and decide whether to reveal the truth or protect themselves at all costs. With a mix of rock music and haunting scatting, the story takes unexpected twists and turns, revealing a web of deceit and betrayal that threatens to tear their lives apart.
