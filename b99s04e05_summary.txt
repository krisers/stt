In a hilarious workplace comedy, the Brooklyn Nine-Nine squad embarks on a high-stakes Halloween Heist to determine the ultimate detective/genius. With unexpected twists, rivalries, and disguises, Assistant Captain Holt reveals a deeper motive behind the competition, leading to a surprising reveal and a plaque rename. As friendships are tested and secrets are uncovered, the team learns the importance of unity and camaraderie during this wild night of hijinks.
