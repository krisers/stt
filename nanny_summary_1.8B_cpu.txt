Synopsis: The play "Romeo and Juliet" is being rewritten by Fran, a nurse turned playwright, with the intention of making it a comedy, replacing the tragic ending with a lighter tone. However, the decision to remove certain scenes is met with resistance from her husband Niles, who feels that she should stay in the play for the benefit of the children and maintain a sense of connection with her parents. Fran, determined to create a new interpretation of the classic tale, engages in a series of misadventures with Niles, leading to unexpected twists and humor. As the production unfolds, the audience watches as the characters navigate their lives and relationships, ultimately finding joy and acceptance in their personal choices. The play ends with a touch of redemption, as the characters return to their respective homes, ready for whatever adventures await them.
