The Halloween Heist is a three-way tiebreaker to determine the champion of champions. Jake Peralta is set to reveal the plaque of destiny, a symbol of the ultimate detective/genius, while his team members must protect the heist and find the real plaque in a mysterious location. The heist involves stealing a plaque from Santiago, a former FBI agent who has been hiding a secret cache containing evidence related to a crime that occurred years ago. After uncovering the truth about Stacey, a rival baby-sitter whose behavior became increasingly suspicious, the team must navigate a series of challenges and comedic moments to secure the plaque and expose Santiago's identity. As the night unfolds, tensions rise and unexpected twists ensue, creating suspense and tension for the audience.
