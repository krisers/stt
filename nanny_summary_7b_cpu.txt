In a comical and dramatic turn, Fran and Niles, a couple, secretly plan to attend a weekend getaway to the Hamptons, despite their contrasting attitudes towards parental responsibilities and a secret romantic subplot. Fran, eager for a break, pretends to be interested in an acting opportunity, while Niles, a butler, is secretly involved in a subversive reinterpretation of Shakespeare's "Romeo and Juliet." Meanwhile, tensions rise at home as Fran's father disapproves of her desire to leave and suspects her involvement in a failed production. Will Fran's theatrical ambitions lead to a confrontation or a chance at personal growth? Find out in this entertaining peek into their lives.
