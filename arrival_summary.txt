In a world on the brink of global panic after 12 mysterious objects land worldwide, linguist Dr. Louise Banks races against time to communicate with the extraterrestrial beings, believing their arrival holds the key to understanding. As she navigates political intrigue and personal revelations, she forms a connection with fellow scientist Ian Donnelly, while deciphering the aliens' cryptic messages, questioning the nature of humanity, and the true purpose of their visit. Their journey uncovers a non-linear perception of time, and the possibility of a shared destiny.
