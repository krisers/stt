In the gripping drama "Law & Order SVU," a fast-food restaurant, HappiBurger, becomes the scene of a heinous crime when a teenage girl is sexually assaulted under the false pretense of a police investigation. As Detectives Benson and Stabler delve deeper, they uncover a complex web of manipulation, deception, and a psychological experiment gone awry, leading them to confront the blurred lines between authority and personal morality. The trial of the accused, Merritt Rook, challenges the jury's beliefs and forces them to question their own compliance with authority.
