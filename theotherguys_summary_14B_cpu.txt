In a high-stakes world of law and chaos in New York City, detectives Danson and Highsmith navigate dangerous assignments and a blurred line between justice and excess. When the iconic duo is replaced after a daring jump, their absence prompts their colleagues, Gamble and Hoitz, to step up and potentially become the city's next unlikely heroes. With a mix of humor, action, and personal struggles, the story follows the duo's pursuit of justice, a high-profile kidnapping, and the complex web of corporate fraud they uncover.
